Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions

Is digital gold the same as physical gold?

Digital gold represents ownership of physical gold that is stored in secure vaults. Each gram of digital gold you purchase is backed by an equivalent amount of 24K 99.9% pure physical gold.

How is my investment secured?

Your digital gold and silver investments are backed by physical metals stored in secure, insured vaults. The physical metals are audited regularly to ensure 100% backing of all digital holdings.

Can I get physical delivery of my gold/silver?

Yes, you can request physical delivery of your gold or silver once you accumulate a minimum quantity (10 grams for gold, 100 grams for silver). Delivery charges and making charges will apply.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through popular digital wallets in Nepal including eSewa, Khalti, IMEPay, and ConnectIPS. You can also use bank transfers and credit/debit cards.

Why do I need to complete KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required by regulations to prevent fraud, money laundering, and ensure the security of all users on our platform. It helps us verify your identity and protect your investments.
